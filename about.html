About Us

Empowering Rural Communities Through Education

Instruct Space

INSTRUCT SPACE is dedicated to empowering rural communities through sustainable education infrastructure development. Our core mission is to bridge the gap in educational access and quality in rural areas by building and rehabilitating schools, providing learning materials, and creating safe, conducive environments for children to thrive.

We focus on long-term solutions that create lasting positive change, helping children unlock their full potential and build brighter futures.

Our Mission

Empowering kids with quality schools and thriving communities to enable them to achieve their full potential.

Our Vision

To ensure every child, regardless of their background, has access to an environment that fosters academic success.

Our Leadership

Our CEO/Founder, Teddy Murithi, leads the Instruct Space Initiative with a clear vision and commitment to community empowerment. Supported by an expert executive team, Teddy shapes our strategy to enable communities to become the architects of their own change. Together, we are dedicated to sustainable, impactful solutions.
