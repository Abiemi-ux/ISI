A Selfless Brother's Struggle

In a small village in Meru County, two young brothers, Ronny and Max, struggle to survive in an environment that seems to offer them no hope. They live with their ailing grandmother and their father, who, due to long-term unemployment, is unable to provide for the family.

For Ronny, life has always been about taking care of his younger brother and doing whatever it takes to keep the family together. But recently, his selfless devotion took a heartbreaking toll when he collapsed from hunger and exhaustion, leaving the family even more vulnerable than before.

A Family Torn Apart by Hardship

The family’s story is one of resilience in the face of overwhelming adversity. Ronny’s father, the sole provider for the household, was unable to complete his degree due to lack of school fees and has struggled to find stable work. Their grandmother, once the pillar of support, is now too weak to help, leaving Ronny to step into the role of caretaker.

With no steady income, food has become a rare luxury. The family often skips meals despite their father’s desperate attempts to secure work. The situation turned critical when Ronny, exhausted from looking after his brother and grandmother while going without food himself, collapsed from hunger.

A Cry for Help from Ronny’s Father

“Ronny is the man of the house. He’s been doing everything he can to take care of his grandmother and brother when I’m absent. I don’t know how he kept going for so long. But when he fell, it was like everything collapsed, and I realized I had to be around.” — James, Ronny’s Father

The family needs urgent help. Their home is in disrepair, and their food supply is dangerously low. Your support can make a difference in their lives.

How Your Support Can Help

  • Provide food and daily necessities to ensure the family has enough to eat.
  • Help with medical care for their grandmother, including medication and supplies.
  • Contribute to building a safe and secure home for them.

A Community That Can Make a Difference

This family has endured more than anyone should have to, but they are not asking for a handout. They are asking for a chance to rebuild their lives.

How You Can Help

Please consider donating today. Your generosity can give Ronny, Max, and their grandmother a fighting chance for a better tomorrow. Every contribution, big or small, makes a difference.

No child should have to sacrifice their health and happiness for survival. Together, we can ensure these two brothers have the chance to be kids again and that their family can find hope amidst the struggle.
